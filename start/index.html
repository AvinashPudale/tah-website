Getting started with Tah

Requirements
  • Tah
  • Android or iOS device

Step 1: Power up the Tah

Connect the Tah to any USB port. It can be on your PC, laptop or Mac. You can also connect the Tah to a USB wall adapter, like the one you use with your smartphone. Make sure that the red LED is always on and the blue LED is blinking.

Step 2: Install the application

Step 3: Connect to the Tah

Scan for BLE devices using the Tah app and connect to the device named Tah

Step 4: Control the LED

We have a green LED connected to pin 13. Toggle the state of pin 13 to turn on/off the green the LED.
